What's been locked in v2.

Every meaningful call this kit makes is logged here. Re-open one only if a new constraint forces it.

D01
Font system: Fraunces (serif) + Karla (sans).
Replaces Cormorant Garamond + Inter from v1. Inter flagged as the #1 AI-generated-content tell; Cormorant Garamond also creeping into AI tools. Fraunces is a distinctive variable serif by Undercase Type — more characterful, less common. Karla is a humanist sans by Jonathan Pinhorn, not on any AI-bait list. Both free on Google Fonts, both work in Canva. See the six options that were considered →
Locked · 2026-05-12
D02
Photography: hybrid B&W + warm muted colour.
Interview portraits (inside a Story Set: Cover, Pull quote, Portrait) are B&W. Everything else (Atmospheric, non-interview content) stays in warm muted colour. Goop precedent — the visual cue separates "this is the real interview" from the rest of the feed without needing a label. Forces specificity in light and gesture, easier to keep consistent across iPhone shots.
Locked · 2026-05-12
D03
Pillar signposting: none.
No pillar tag, label, or icon on any post. Content, format, and visual treatment do the categorising. The feed teaches the system over the first few posts. Pillar tracking (Identity / Work / Relationships / The Unsaid) happens internally — in Notion, in the editorial calendar — not on the post itself.
Locked · 2026-05-12
D04
Wordmark: Fraunces, stacked caps.
THIRD / SPACE on two lines, Fraunces Medium 500, 0.18em tracking, Ink on Bone. Considered: a Karla wide-spaced masthead. Stacked Fraunces wins on distinctiveness — the serif carries the editorial register and the stacking is unusual enough in the SEA market to read as ours. See it rendered →
