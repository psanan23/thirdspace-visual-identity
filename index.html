What this is

A rulebook, not a moodboard.

Third Space lives on Instagram first. The job of this kit is to keep every post recognisable as Third Space — whether you, a designer, or an AI tool makes it. If it's in here, it's a rule. If it's not, it's still being decided.

For you

Open it before you post.

Five colours, two fonts, five post types, one feed pattern. If a draft doesn't follow them, redo the draft — not the rules.

For collaborators

Send the link, skip the brief.

Anyone helping with content — a designer, a writer, a photographer — should be able to open this and get to work without a meeting.

For AI tools

Paste the brief, get on-brand output.

The Brief an AI page gives you a ready-to-paste prompt for Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney, or any image tool.

Brand belief

Modern women are not choosing between ambition, motherhood, identity, and desire. They are learning how to hold all of it.

Every visual choice in this kit ladders up to that belief. Quiet, considered, honest. Editorial, not aspirational. Specific, not abstract.

She is more than the roles she performs.

Never reduce her to mother, founder, or wife alone.

Ambition changes shape. It doesn't disappear.

Show the deliberateness behind any move, not just the move.

Style, work, money, relationships are one life.

The visual system treats them as one. No category badges, no lifestyle silos.

Foundations at a glance

The fixed parts of the system.

Borrowed from Spotify's social style guide: a brand has mandatory elements (don't break) and flexible elements (room to play). Here's what's mandatory for Third Space.

Mandatory

Two fonts.

Fraunces + Inter. Nothing else. See type →

Mandatory

Five colours.

Bone, Ink, Tobacco, Honey, Blush. No bright colour, no pure white. See palette →

Mandatory

Five post types.

Cover, Pull quote, Portrait, Atmospheric, Editor's note. See posts →

Mandatory

One feed pattern.

Every 9 posts hit all 5 types. No two pull quotes side by side. See feed →

Decisions log: Every meaningful call this kit makes — fonts, photography treatment, wordmark direction, pillar signposting — is logged with reasoning at /decisions. Don't undo a locked decision without writing a new entry that explains why.
