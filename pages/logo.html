Wordmark rules

Six rules, no exceptions.

  1. Fraunces Medium 500, all caps, stacked on two lines: THIRD / SPACE. Nothing else is the wordmark.
  2. Tracking is 0.18em. Line-height is 1. Never adjust these.
  3. The wordmark goes in Ink on Bone, or Bone on Ink. No other colour.
  4. No icon, no monogram, no swirl, no symbol next to it.
  5. No tagline next to the wordmark. The IG bio holds the line.
  6. The wordmark only appears on Cover posts and the IG profile. Not on every post.
