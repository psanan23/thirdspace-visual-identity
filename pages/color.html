02 — Color

Five colours, two backups.

Warm and quiet. None of them are pure black, pure white, or bright. Get this right and the rest of the system holds.

The palette

What each colour is for.

Bone and Ink do most of the work. Tobacco carries second-tier text. Honey and Blush are the moments of life.

Color rules

Don't break these.

  1. Two out of every three posts use Bone as the background.
  2. Honey + Tobacco is great. Honey + Blush is great. Don't invent other combinations.
  3. Never use more than three colours on a single post.
  4. No pure white. No pure black. No bright colour. Ever.
  5. Blush is for featured-story moments only. Max one in nine posts.
