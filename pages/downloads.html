Fonts

Fraunces + Karla.

Both free on Google Fonts. The link below is the same one whether you're styling a website, a Canva post, or installing on your laptop. On Google Fonts, click "Get font" to download / install.

Serif · big text
Fraunces
Headlines, pull quotes, editor's notes, the wordmark. Variable serif by Undercase Type. Pick weights 300, 400, 500 + italic 400.
Get on Google Fonts ↗
Sans · small text
Karla
Body, captions, attribution. Humanist sans-serif by Jonathan Pinhorn. Pick weights 400 + 500.
Get on Google Fonts ↗
For Canva specifically
Search inside Canva
Canva has both fonts indexed under their exact names. Type "Fraunces" or "Karla" into the font picker. Free plan supports both.
